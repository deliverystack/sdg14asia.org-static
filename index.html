Who We Are

Dedicated to Life Below Water

The SDG14 Asia Association is dedicated to supporting initiatives that protect and sustainably manage oceans and marine ecosystems across Asia. We mobilize resources, encourage scientific collaboration, and support projects that preserve marine biodiversity while promoting sustainable development.
